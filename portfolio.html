My Approach

A structured, iterative process that ensures dashboards deliver real business value.

1

Discovery & Requirements

Collaborate with stakeholders to understand business objectives, key metrics, and decision-making needs. Define success criteria and establish clear deliverables.

2

Data Modeling

Design efficient star schema data models with proper relationships, create comprehensive date dimensions, and optimize for performance. Ensure data quality and integrity.

3

DAX Development

Build advanced measures including time intelligence, running totals, rankings, and custom calculations. Organize measures logically and document complex formulas.

4

Visual Design

Create intuitive, accessible visualizations following best practices. Apply consistent color schemes, typography, and layout principles. Ensure mobile responsiveness.

5

Interactivity & Testing

Implement drill-through pages, bookmarks, and dynamic filtering. Conduct thorough testing across different scenarios and data selections. Optimize performance.

6

Deployment & Training

Deploy to Power BI Service, configure refresh schedules, and set up appropriate security. Provide user training and comprehensive documentation.
