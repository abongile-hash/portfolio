## 🎯 What You'll Build

A **5-page interactive sales dashboard** featuring:

### Page 1: Executive Summary
- KPI cards for key metrics (Revenue, Orders, AOV, Margin)
- Revenue trend line chart with 3-month moving average
- Product category breakdown (donut chart)
- Top 5 products table
- Sales by territory visualization

### Page 2: Product Performance
- Comprehensive product performance table (top 20)
- Treemap showing product hierarchy
- Scatter chart for price vs volume analysis
- Product category and subcategory filters

### Page 3: Customer Insights
- Customer metrics (total, revenue per customer)
- Top 10 customers ranking table
- Customer distribution by territory
- Territory performance matrix

### Page 4: Sales Team Performance
- Salesperson leaderboard with full metrics
- Revenue by salesperson comparison
- Territory performance analysis
- Team productivity KPIs

### Page 5: Time Analysis
- YTD and MTD revenue tracking
- Running total revenue visualization
- Year-over-year comparison charts
- Monthly performance trends

---

## 📊 Measures Provided

**26 pre-built DAX measures** organized into 8 categories:

1. **Core Metrics** (5) - Revenue, Orders, AOV, Quantity, Products
2. **Profitability** (5) - Cost, Profit, Margin, Freight, Tax
3. **Time Intelligence** (7) - PY, YoY, YTD, MTD, Running Total, Moving Avg
4. **Customer Metrics** (2) - Total Customers, Revenue per Customer
5. **Product Metrics** (3) - Unit Price, Discount %, Products Sold
6. **Sales Channel** (3) - Online/Offline Orders, Channel Mix
7. **Sales Team** (3) - Salespeople, Revenue per Salesperson, Territories
8. **Advanced Analytics** (3) - Ranking, % Contribution, Top N

All measures are already created in your Power BI model in the **_Measures** table.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

**Issue**: Measures not calculating correctly
- **Solution**: Check relationship directions, verify active relationships
- **Reference**: implementation_steps.md, Phase 9

**Issue**: Visuals loading slowly
- **Solution**: Apply Top N filters, reduce visual count per page
- **Reference**: dashboard_template_guide.md, Performance section

**Issue**: Date sorting incorrectly
- **Solution**: Sort Month Name by Date column, not alphabetically
- **Reference**: quick_reference.md, Troubleshooting

**Issue**: Drill-through not working
- **Solution**: Verify drill-through fields configured on target page
- **Reference**: implementation_steps.md, Phase 7
